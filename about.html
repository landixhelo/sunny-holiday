ABOUT THE SUNNY HOLIDAY

Escape to a World of Sunny Apartments

Sun Holiday is the open place to relax. Each apartment is brand new and comes with a private bathroom, private kitchen and a spacious private balcony overlooking the sea. It is perfect if you want peace and quiet and to be near the beach.

The owners have many years of schooling taking care of guest villas in Corfu, Greece, so the owners are no strangers to customer service and cleanliness. They have now opened their own small business in Saranda, Albania and are always available and available to be with anything you may need or any information you need to know.
